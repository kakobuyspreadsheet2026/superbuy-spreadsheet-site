Seven steps before you ship internationally

  1. Land on the grid — home first; search if you are not in a scrolling mood.
  2. Click through — let MaisonLooks own the details; our card is only a preview.
  3. Interrogate the listing — size charts, stock flags, and fine print beat the tile’s vibe.
  4. Paste into GTBuy — match their expected format; screenshots are backup, not primary input.
  5. Fund on gtbuy.com — cards, wallets, top-ups—whatever GTBuy allows, inside their UI.
  6. Warehouse photos — approve or request retakes; this is your last cheap exit.
  7. Ship — choose a line, declare, pray to the customs gods responsibly.

What to double-check before you pay

  • Seller notes — read the fine print on pre-sale, processing time, and color variance.
  • Size tolerance — measure twice; factory tolerances are not boutique tolerances.
  • Version drift — “same link” can mean different factory weeks; ask if unsure.
  • Insurance — decide whether you need add-ons before the parcel leaves China.
  • Payment timing — know what triggers non-refundable charges on your agent.

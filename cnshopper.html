How to use this site, then buy on CnShopper (or any agent)

  1. Browse — infinite scroll until something stops your thumb.
  2. Deep-link — new tab, full context—no iframe cosplay.
  3. Verify — if the listing contradicts the tile, trust the listing.
  4. Submit — CnShopper’s form, not a Google Form from a stranger.
  5. Pay — only where SSL and your saved bookmark say cnshopper.com.
  6. Inspect QC — pixels cost money; zoom them.
  7. Export — pick a route, insure if needed, track like your money depends on it—because it does.

What to double-check before you pay

  • Seller notes — read the fine print on pre-sale, processing time, and color variance.
  • Size tolerance — measure twice; factory tolerances are not boutique tolerances.
  • Version drift — “same link” can mean different factory weeks; ask if unsure.
  • Insurance — decide whether you need add-ons before the parcel leaves China.
  • Payment timing — know what triggers non-refundable charges on your agent.
Safety tip. Phishing loves agent keywords. Bookmark cnshopper.com from a source you trust; never trust a “shortcut” spreadsheet that asks for passwords.

Before you blame the agent

  • Assuming English titles match factory reality — read measurements and material lines.
  • Chasing the lowest line price — shipping and service fees can erase the win.
  • Shipping hype items with the slowest line — balance risk and speed deliberately.
  • Ignoring seller processing times — pre-orders and holidays exist.
  • Using the same password everywhere — isolate your agent credentials.

Rough timeline (order → door)

Expect order → domestic transit → warehouse → QC → outbound → customs → delivery, with seasonal spikes. If your parcel feels stuck, refresh tracking on CnShopper, not on this page—we do not have tracking.
